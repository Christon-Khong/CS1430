Top Ten Signs You Have Been Spending Too Much Time Web Surfing

  1. You're deep into a Youtube video you never would have ever searched for
  2. Way too many tabs open on your browser
  3. You speak in memes
  4. You've memorized your credit card number, expiration date, and security code
  5. Your wi-fi being down sends you into a fit of rage
  6. You say "L-O-L" or "O-M-G"
  7. You say things to people online that you wouldn't in person
  8. You believe the earth is flat
  9. You think you're smarter than the doctor
  10. Your dying wish is to have your browsing history deleted
